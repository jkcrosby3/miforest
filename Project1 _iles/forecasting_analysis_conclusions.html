FORECASTING, ANALYSES, CONCLUSIONS

Analysis of the Educational, Healthcare, and Military Spending of the G20 Countries

Identify any interesting and useful relationships and trends

While the USA appears to remain strong, it appears like certain countries are doing well with GDP, Healthcare, and Education. Taking better care of their citizens in an indicator of a stronger economy. The question I've had as I have looked at this is why certain countries with a large GDP don't appear to spend much on education or healthcare. I wonder if it is because the citizens utilize different methods of healthcare than modern medicine, so lowering the cost to the government? Also, is larger expenditure really an indicator of better healthcare or education?

Forecasting, Analyses, Conclusions

After looking at the charts, I anticipate that Saudi Arabia and China will come closer in military spending to that of the USA. They definitely need to re-evaluate their education and healthcare spending though if they want the country, as a whole, to increase in performance and health, and thus increase their standing as a whole worldwide.
